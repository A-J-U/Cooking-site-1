Korean Garlic Chicken

Korean Garlic Chicken

Info

Time
45 Min
Difficulty
Servings
2

Ingredients

2
Chicken Breast
1
2 Cups Tteok (Korean Rice Cake)
1
Salt and Pepper To Taste
1
1 Tsp. Garlic Powder
1
1 Tsp. Onion Powder
1
1 Bunch Scallions
1
2 Tbs. Unsalted Butter
2
Cloves Minced Garlic
1
1 Tbs. Shoyu or Soy Sauce
1
2 Tbs. Vinegar
1
5 Tbs. Honey
2
2 Tbs. Vegetable Oil
1
Medium Onion Sliced

Directions

  1. Make the sauce, Combined the Soy Sauce, Vinegar, and Honey in a bowl and mix.
  2. Cut the chicken breast into pinky sized strips and season with salt, Pepper, Onion and Garlic Powder.
  3. Prepare the Rice Cakes, Heat a pot of water over high heat until boiling then add in the Tteok.
  4. Cover the Pot and let the Tteok cook until tender in the middle, About 6 Minutes.
  5. Heat a pan over medium heat and add in the Vegetable Oil.
  6. Once the oil starts to shimmer add in the Minced Garlic and Onion, Cook until fragrent (1-2 Min).
  7. Remove the Onion and set aside in a bowl, Then add in the sliced Chicken.
  8. Brown the chicken eavenly on all sides then add in the Onions again.
  9. Drain the Tteok and add them to the pan with the chicken and Onions.
  10. Finally add in the sauce and toss all the ingredients together until the sauce reduces and becomes thicker.
  11. Add the Butter in to give the sauce a richer flavor and texture.
  12. Serve on a plate with Scallions on top.
  13. (Optional) Serve with rice.
