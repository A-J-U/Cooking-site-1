Mushroom Soup

Info

Time
1 Hr
Difficulty
Servings
4

Ingredients

2
Lbs. Mushrooms of your choice (button, cremini, portabello, or shiitake).
4
Tbs. Unsalted Butter
1
Medium Onion
4
Cloves Garlic
2
Tbs. Flour
1
Cup White Wine
1
Cup Milk
5
Cups low sodium Chicken Broth
2
Bay Leaves
2
Sprigs of Time
1
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions

  1. Prepare your Mushrooms, Slice them into small chunks and set aside.
  2. Mince the Garlic and Finely chop the Onion.
  3. In a large pot add in your Butter and Melt over medium high heat.
  4. Add in you Mushrooms and cook until the liquid from the Mushrooms has evaporated (about 10 Min).
  5. Add the Onion and Garlic then cook until softened and fragrent.
  6. Add the Flour and stir to combined.
  7. Once the Flour is combined Deglaze with White Wine and stir until the Wine is reduced by half.
  8. When the Wine is reduced by half add in your Milk, Chicken Broth, Bay Leaves, and Thyme and stir until combined.
  9. Cook on a Medium Low heat stirring occasionally for about 20 Min.
  10. Remove the Bay Leaves and Thyme with Tongs and blend the soup with an immersion blender until smooth.
  11. Season with more salt and pepper and optionally add in some lemon.
  12. Optionally garnish with Terragon, Chives or Parsley.
  13. Serve with Toasted Bread or Crostini.
