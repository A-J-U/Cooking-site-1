Cacio E Pepe

Info

Time
30 Min
Difficulty
Servings
2

Ingredients

1
200g (7oz) Spaghetti
1
5 Oz. Pancetta
1
Tbs. Black Peppercorns
3
Tbs. Olive Oil
1
Tsps. Black Truffle Oil
1
3/4 Cup Pecorino Romano
1
3/4 Cup Parmigiano Reggiano

Directions

  1. Start by preparing your Pancetta, Cheese, and your Peppercorns.
  2. Slice your Pancetta into small bite sized pieces and set aside.
  3. Grate your Cheeses on the fine side of a box grater and place in a bowl.
  4. Grind your Peppercorns in a Mortar and Pestle.
  5. In a large pot bring 6 Cups of salted water to a boil.
  6. Heat a pan to medium and sear your Pancetta until crisp and the fat has rendered.
  7. Once your Pancetta has finished set aside but leave the rendered fat in the pan.
  8. Add your Pasta to the water and cook until al dente (about 1 Min. less than the package reccomends).
  9. Add the Ground Peppercorns to the Pancetta fat and toast on a low heat until fragrant.
  10. When the Pasta is al dente transfer to the pan with the Pepper and Fat.
  11. When transfering the Pasta make sure to add about 1/4 cup of pasta water to the pan as well.
  12. Now to make the sauce, Combined your Cheeses in a bowl and add in 1/3 cup of pasta water and whisk to combined until a smooth sauce is achieved.
  13. Add your sauce to the pan and toss until the sauce and fat from the pancetta are emulsified.
  14. Add your Black Truffle Oil just before serving.
  15. Serve and enjoy.
