Dashi Stock Eggs

Time
15 Minutes
Difficulty
Servings
1

Ingredients

2
Eggs
1
1/4 Cup Dashi Stock
1
1 Tbs. Vegetable Oil
1
Bunch Scallions to taste
1
Pinch of Garlic Powder
1
Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. To make dashi stock add 2 Tsp. to 1/4 Cup water.
  2. add oil to pan and preheat to medium high.
  3. Once oil is shimmering crack in both eggs.
  4. Wait until the whites have just set then season with Salt, Pepper, and Garlic Powder.
  5. After seasoning add in dashi stock around eggs and cover with a lid.
  6. Wait 1-2 Min, or until dashi stock has almost entirely cooked off.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of Soy Sauce and Scallions on top.
  8. (Optional) Toast Bread and spread a light layer of miso paste on top, Add the eggs and serve.
