Pho

Info

Time
1 H
Difficulty
Servings
3

Ingredients

2
Tbs. Beef Bouillon
1
Tbs. Fish Sauce
1
Tbs. Sugar
1
2 inch Piece of Ginger
1
Yellow Onion
2
Cloves
2
Star Anise
1
1/2 Lb. Rice Stick Noodles
1
1/2 Lb. Beef Strip Loin Thinly Sliced
1
Cup Vietnamese Basil Leaves
1
Cup Bean Sprouts
1
1/2 Cup Cilantro Leaves
4
Scallions
1
Lime
1
Pinch of Salt

Directions

  1. Start by making the aromatics satchel. Wrap the Star Anise, Cloves, and Cinnamon Stick in cheese cloth and tie with butchers twine.
  2. Prepare your Onion and Ginger, Slice the Ginger into thin strips. Slice the Onion in half and slice that half into thin rings, Set both aside.
  3. Slice the Scallion into small 1/8 Inch pieces and put in a bowl covered with water.
  4. In a large pot add in 1 Quart of water and the Beef Bouillon then bring that to a boil.
  5. Add the Fish Sauce, Sugar, Ginger, Onion and salt to the stock and reduce the heat to a simmer.
  6. Add in the aromatics satchel and boil for 30 Min.
  7. Once 30 Min is up remove the satchel and taste for seasoning then prepare the noodles.
  8. In a medium pot bring water to a boil.
  9. Take the other half of the onion you have and slice very thinly into half circles.
  10. Once a rolling boil is achieved add in your noodles and cook until just al dente (10 - 20 Seconds).
  11. When the noodles are al dente add them to a bowl, Place your sliced beef on top of the noodles.
  12. Add your garnishes: Bean Sprouts, Cilantro, Basil, Onion Slices, and Scallions.
  13. Using a ladle, Cover the Beef and garnishes with broth.
  14. Garnish with a Lime Wedge and Serve.
